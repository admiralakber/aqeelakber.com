Aqeel Akber

I am Aqeel Akber, a Ph.D dropout in nuclear physics at the Australian National University.

I founded a company.
I exited that company.

I will always be a scientist.
I love art, science, nature, and humanity; a lot.
Through their stories and being:
My Father taught me to be a scientist is to be a humble servant to society;
and much more.
My Mother taught me to hold space, make space, to be patient, to work hard, with fire and alacrity.
My Friend and Mentor taught me .. pretty much everything.

I am the product of all my experiences; including publishing these words.
I believe in living the future you wish to see.
I founded my personal research company.
I create for-profit companies and startups to make the change I want to see in the world.
I strive to make the economic decision the ethical one.
I will speak, consult, advise, help, etc, for a fee and if there is mutual aligned benefit.
I have a LinkedIn, BlueSky, Instagram, X
I enjoy making machines that help me achieve my goals for fun and profit; to that end,
I am one of those people who have been coding and using *unix forever.
I live contemporary. I'm proud of my ability to learn. I am inspired by existence.
I operate within this system.

I have pride in my queerness.
I will always find beauty.
This blog is a brain dump. Enjoy.
